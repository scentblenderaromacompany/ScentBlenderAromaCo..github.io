Our Shipping Policy

We strive to deliver the best shipping options, no matter where you live. Here is everything you need to know about our shipping policy:

  • Processing Time: All orders are processed within 24-48 hours of placement.
  • Shipping Methods: We offer various shipping methods to meet your needs, from standard to expedited shipping.
  • Shipping Costs: Shipping costs vary depending on the destination and the shipping method selected. Free shipping is available for orders over a certain amount.
  • International Shipping: We ship internationally but please be aware that additional charges, such as customs fees, may apply for international orders.
  • Delivery Times: Delivery times vary based on location and shipping method. Typically, domestic orders take 3-5 business days to arrive, while international orders may take 7-15 business days.
  • Tracking Information: After your order is shipped, you will receive an email with tracking information so you can track your order every step of the way.

If you have any questions about shipping, please contact us at support@scentblenderaroma.com.
