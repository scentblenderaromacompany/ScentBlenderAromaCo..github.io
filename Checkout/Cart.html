How Your Cart Works

Items remain in your cart for 120 minutes to ensure availability. After this period, they may be removed if not purchased.

Here are some features to enhance your shopping experience:

  • Save for Later: Decide what you want now and save other items for future purchase without losing them.
  • Price Alerts: Get notified if the prices of items in your cart change.
  • Easy Checkout: Securely save your payment and shipping options for a faster and smoother checkout experience.
