Our Returns Policy

At Scent Blender Aroma Company, your satisfaction is our priority. Here’s how you can return a product if you're not completely happy with your purchase:

  • Products can be returned within 30 days of purchase.
  • The product must be in its original condition, unused, and with all packaging intact.
  • To initiate a return, please contact our support team at support@scentblenderaroma.com.

Refund Policy

Once your return is received and inspected, here’s what you can expect:

  • If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your credit card or original method of payment within a certain amount of days.
  • Shipping costs are non-refundable. If you receive a refund, the cost of return shipping will be deducted from your refund.

Have more questions? Our customer service team is here to help: support@scentblenderaroma.com
